What is Project Match maker

Project match maker would be offering to find a partner according to thier skills so it would be benefits to each and every group and they can perform well in group project and also get some good marks.

Project match maker has one significant advantage that group leader show every students profile, when he got matched and he can alsp cancel the match or delete their profile form match list. It is easy for group leader to select a perfect group partner. it can solve the problem and also saved a lot time for student and group leader.

Using match making function will increase the number of studnets reqeusts per day as it will be very easy for them to find a perfect group partner with their required skills.

When the students having problem with the sign-up and login, they directly contact to admin section, and they can solve it with in a few hours.
